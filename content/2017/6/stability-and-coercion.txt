<p>I didn't write a thesis, but one of the more significant papers I wrote in college was "Stability and Coercion" for the course "Contemporary Political Theory" taught by <a href=https://scholar.harvard.edu/michaelrosen>Michael Rosen</a>. My views have updated somewhat since 2013, but this is still a reasonably good outline of my criticisms with much of contemporary political thought.</p>
