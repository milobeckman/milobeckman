Music I liked in 2017
at 3:28 PM on Jan 9, 2018   |   filed under music

Here are my 100 most-played tracks (most-played at the top) according to Spotify, which says I listened to about an hour of music per day in 2017. This list excludes SoundCloud, so it probably undercounts this album and this song, which were the main things I listened to on SoundCloud.

My favorite albums this year in no particular order were Fin by Syd, SATURATION II by BROCKHAMPTON, and DAMN. by Kendrick. I also was shown for the first time Arular by MIA and Mama's Gun by Erykah Badu, which were in a different sense my favorite albums of 2017.
