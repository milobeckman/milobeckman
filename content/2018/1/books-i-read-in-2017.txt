<p>My new years resolution in 2017 was to read 40 books by the end of the year, which ended up being one of my main sources of joy/sanity this year.</p>

<p>This is the list of every book I finished. I started a lot more books than I finished, so any book on that made it here is at least some level of good, interesting, informative, somehow worthwhile. But my favorites (the ones that stuck with me or significantly changed the way I think) are in bold -- all strongly recommended!</p>

<p>I'm planning at some point to write a little summary or post about each of the bold books, but I'm planning to do a lot of things so for now here's the list...</p>
