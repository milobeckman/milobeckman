Who am I?
updated at 10:24 PM on Nov 2, 2016   |   filed under administrative

I'm Milo, a math educator, freelance journalist, and software developer living in New York. I'm an alum of Stuyvesant High School and Harvard University, and have worked in the past at Morgan Stanley, the Office of Sen. Kirsten Gillibrand, Jane Street Capital, TruMid Financial LLC, and others. Currently, I am self-employed as a math/science tutor for Forum Education and math team coach for Bridge to Enter Advanced Mathematics, a freelance writer for FiveThirtyEight and Shareblue, a crossword constructor for the New York Times, and occasionally some other things.
