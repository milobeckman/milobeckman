I create "mini" and "midi" sized crossword puzzles for the New York Times crossword app. They're available for download in the "Packs" section of the app (fyi they cost $3.99). Here are the packs I've made so far:

  • 2019 — Wizarding Midis, with Portia Lundie (20 puzzles)
  • 2018 — Themeless Midis 2 (20 puzzles)
  • 2018 — Music Midis (20 puzzles)
  • 2017 — Modern Minis 4 (30 puzzles)
  • 2017 — Mini Mania 9 (30 puzzles)
